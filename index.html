About

I'm a 4th-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the Arizona State University. I work at Cognition & Intellifent Lab, supervised by Dr. Chitta Baral. My research lies in natural language processing and deep learning. Specifically, I'm interested in open domain question answering and open domain retrieval under multimodal settings. Recently, I am also exploring open-domain dialogue application.
