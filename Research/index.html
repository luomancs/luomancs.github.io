Manuscript Simulation Based on RNNs




The Project I participated in is developing a Data Processing & Analyzing Platform for Medicare data in Beijing. There are more than 20 million patients and nearly 5 billion medical treatment data collected during last decade, which is too enormous for working staff to manipulate these data. This project is aimed at developing a platform to satisfy the demands of checking, summarizing, analyzing these data, and supervising the enactment of Medicare policies. The project could be divided into the four major parts: extracting current data from database into Hadoop based Big Data platform, predictive modeling, Spark based distributed-processing, and data visualizing.
