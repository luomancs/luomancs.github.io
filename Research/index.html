Research

2017

Handwriting Simulation Based on RNNs

The main purpose of this project is predicting and simulating different styles of handwriting. The training data come from IAM-OnDB, consisting of more than 900 volunteers' drafs.

Based on Spring and Hibernate framwork, I designed a user-friendly management system of experiment course. Users can manipulate add, delete, edit and so on operations to manage their experiments in schools.

Smart growth of City

This project came from Mathematical Contest in Modeling of the United States 2017. We used linear regression approach and collected city data from google map to predicate the development of city and finally gave smart-growth plans for two cities based on the result of our analysis

2016

Speech Recognition With Weighted Finite-State Transducers

The aim of this project was analysing huge amount of speech data with samll storage spaces. The idea was deviding the orignal data into several small pieces and finally combined the analized results. This project involved with different speech recognition technique such as GMM, HMM, HLDA, LDA, PCA, BP etc.
